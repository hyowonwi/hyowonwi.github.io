I am a Ph.D. student in the [School of Computing](https://cs.kaist.ac.kr/) at [KAIST](https://www.kaist.ac.kr/), advised by [Prof. Noseong Park](https://noseong.github.io/). Before joining KAIST, I received my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science from [Yonsei University](https://www.yonsei.ac.kr/).

My research interests center on **Transformer architectures** and **parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT)** techniques for **large language models (LLMs)**, with a particular focus on efficiency-oriented design for LLM inference and training.

I am currently an NLP research intern at **NC AI**, contributing to the *World Best LLM* project.
